21st century education


I recently got the chance to see a new middle school in Vancouver which seeks to deviate itself from the traditional closed walls of classrooms and the monotone of hallways. The school opts, instead, for open hallways and clear glass classrooms and an emphasis on student interactions rather than memorization, claiming that they somehow enhance learning.

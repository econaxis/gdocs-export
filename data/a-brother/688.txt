My Brother
Having a brother to play with is always nice, but arguing and fighting with them is not. On countless occasions, my brother and I have fought, argued, and wrestled with one another for the pride, the control, and the good feeling of seeing the other one lose. Many people, including my parents, say that arguing is not good, and try to make harmony, but I partly believe that they are wrong. Arguing means speaking in a way that persuades another to comply with one’s wishes, regardless of the severity. In everyday life, we have to persuade people to comply with our wishes, even if they are not very complaisant. However, arguing is not a very pleasant experience for me, although I admit that it can be very didactic.
